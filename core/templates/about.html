About Kindergarten SMS

The Kindergarten School Management System (SMS) is a modern web-based platform designed to simplify and automate the academic and administrative operations of educational institutions. Built using Django and Bootstrap 5, our system ensures a secure, responsive, and user-friendly experience for all stakeholders.

Whether you're an administrator managing admissions, a teacher tracking homework and attendance, or a student submitting assignments, Kindergarten SMS provides the tools you need in one centralized dashboard.

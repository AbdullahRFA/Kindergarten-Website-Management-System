About Kindergarten SMS

Kindergarten School Management System (SMS) is a comprehensive digital platform designed to streamline every aspect of school operations—from admissions and academics to communication and finance. Built with Django and Bootstrap 5, it offers secure, responsive, and intuitive tools for administrators, teachers, students, and parents.

🎓 Our Process

  • Digital admission and enrollment with automated fee tracking
  • Role-based dashboards for admins, teachers, and students
  • Daily attendance, homework submission, and exam results
  • Internal messaging and notification system
  • Simulated bus tracking for student safety

  • Database: PostgreSQL / SQLite
  • Deployment: PythonAnywhere, Render, or Heroku

Our mission is to empower schools with inclusive, efficient, and scalable tools that allow educators to focus on what truly matters—student growth and lifelong learning.
